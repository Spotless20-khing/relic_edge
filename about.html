About Us

Driving Digital Transformation Across Africa

Relic Edge is a Ghanaian technology company founded with a vision to empower businesses through intelligent software solutions and digital strategy. From custom platforms for hospitals and schools to eCommerce and SaaS development, we deliver digital tools that solve real problems.

What started as a development initiative led by our founder, Eyram Abusah, has evolved into a growing, impact-driven team of software engineers, designers, and tech consultants. Our mission is to build scalable solutions that fuel growth for businesses, institutions, and startups across the continent.
